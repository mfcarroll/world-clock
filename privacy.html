Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy applies to the GeoTime application by Matthew F. Carroll, currently available as a web app and on iOS.

We do not collect, store, or share any personal information whatsoever. The app requests access to your device's location for the sole purpose of displaying the current time and timezone at your location. This location data is used only while the app is active and is never stored on your device or transmitted to our servers or any third party.
